About Me

Hi, my name is Isaac Uili. I am currently a student at Year Up, enrolled in the Software Development, QA & Testing Track.

Growing up I've always been surrounded by video games and electronics. I really enjoy video games and eventually I want to make one as well. The creativity of video games has lead me to programming and the possibilities with it has always piqued my interest.

I've been interested in how to apply programming to my daily life, making code to make everything easier. Utilizing that is just a huge benefit, especially in a digital world like ours.

Over the years I've felt my inspiration to make a video game turning into more things of what I want to do. There is a lot with programming I want to do and learning code is just the start. Over my career I want to have experience with all sorts of code to make myself a better programmer overall.

I see my attendance at Year Up as a huge step in life, by applying myself to coding and being more proactive. Taking every experience with me to the next, I aim for this to be consistent and constantly make myself better than before.
